Know Your Rights!

Just because you cant afford your rent doesn't mean you don't have rights.

Your Next Steps

1. Unfortunately, it is highly unlikely that you will be able to succesfully stop this eviction

2. However, your landlord can not evict you right away.

3. You may wish to file for dispute resolution if 5 days have not passed since you received your notice. If you explain your situation you may be able to buy yourself more time.

4. Even if you do not contest your eviction remember that your landlord and the police cannot evict you - you can only be evicted by a registered bailiff hired by your landlord to evict you
