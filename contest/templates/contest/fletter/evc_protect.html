Worried About an Eviction? Know Your Rights!

If you are concerned that your landlord may try to evict you please read the following carefully and take steps to protect yourself.

Evidence and Witnesses Are Your Best Defense

1. Keep all of your communications with your landlord in writing and keep copies of everything

2. Whenever possible have a reliable witness present when you communicate with your landlord

3. Ask for and retain rent receipts after you pay rent

4. Talk to your neighbors and see if they have similar concerns, if they do support each other!

Never Withhold Rent

1. The easiest way to get evicted is to be late with your rent.

2. No matter how frusturating things get, NEVER withhold your rent.

3. If you need repairs done on your building use this website to start asserting your rights as tenants

4. Talk to your neighbors and see if they have similar concerns, if they do support each other!

Get Organized and Fight Back

1. Talk to your neighbors and start a tenants organization

2. Use this website to share your experience

3. Join a neighborhood renter's union and get involved in local politics

4. Urge your politicians to protect renter's rights and reform the RTA
