Anti-Eviction

Welcome to Anti-Eviction, an online resource for information about tenants rights and the residential tenancy branch in British Columbia.

This website was made by me, Will Damon, a social scientist and community organizer. I am not a lawyer or a trained advocate, although lawyers and advocates have vetted these resources.

These tools are intended to help get you learn about your rights, get organized, and start the process of addressing a residential tenancy issue. Their are advocates available to help, please consult them as early in the process as possible.

  • TRAC (Tenant Resource and Advisory Centre)
  • CLAS (Community Legal Assistance Society)
